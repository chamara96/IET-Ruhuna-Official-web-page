PATW 2019

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) Young Professionals on campus Ruhuna is pleased to announce annual Present-Around-the-World (PATW) competition that will happen on 06th July 2019 (Saturday) 8.30 a.m. Onwards.
PATW is a global competition for young professionals and students within engineering to develop and showcase their presentation skills. Open to IET members and non-members . It is a competition open to applicants between 18 and 30 years old. Individual competitors give a ten minute presentation on a technology or engineering related subject of their choice in front of an audience and a panel of judges, followed by a five minute question and answer session.
