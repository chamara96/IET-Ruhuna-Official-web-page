EVENT 2018

Field Visit - LTL Holdings

• 17th October 2018

As the fifth event of the year 2018, a field visit was organized by the IET YP UoR on 17th October 2018 to the Angulana LTL Transformer factory. The students were expected to gain some industrial experience and knowledge regarding using of transformers in the industry. There were semester 8 students from Electrical and Information Engineering Department.

AutoCAD Electrical Workshop

• 15th October 2018

As the fourth event of the year 2018, an AutoCAD Electrical Workshop was organized by the IET YP UoR on 15th October 2018 in the Computer center of the Department of Interdisciplinary Studies. The main idea of the workshop was to give some basic knowledge about using AutoCAD Electrical and how to draw Electrical designs using the software.

Electronic Design Workshop

• 08th October 2018

As the second event of the year 2018, an Electronic Design Workshop was organized by the IET YP UoR on 8th October 2018 in the Computer center of the Electrical and Information Engineering Department. The main idea of the workshop was to give some basic ideas regarding types of OP Amps such as non-inverting, inverting, summing, Schmitt trigger and voltage follower, give some knowledge regarding how to use MOSFETs as switches and how to use transistors as switches, give some understanding about Optocouplers, give some understanding about LEDs and LDRs and to give some knowledge about how to read voltage, current and frequency using microcontrollers.

PLC workshop

• 11th July 2018

As the first event of the year 2018, a PLC workshop was organized by the IET YP UoR on 11th July 2018 in the Computer center of the Electrical and Information Engineering Department. The main idea of the workshop was to give some basic ideas of PLC such as using XCPro and ladder programming. There were semester 3, 5 and 7 students from Electrical and Mechanical Engineering departments. And all together there were thirty participants for the workshop.

Annual General Meeting - 2018

• 06th of June 2018

On the 06th of June 2018, AGM of the IET YP UoR chapter was held at the Faculty premises. For this occasion, head of the department Dr. Keerthi Gunawickrama, senior lecturer Dr. R.P.S. Chandrasena and lecturer Mr. K.M.S.Y. Konara was participated. The students of the Electrical & Information Engineering Department representing three years also joined this event.
