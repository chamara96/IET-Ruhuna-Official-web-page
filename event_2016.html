EVENT 2016

Telecommunication Workshop & Recruitment Day

• 16th November 2016

The workshop conducted by the IET, Ruhuna chapter was held on 16th of November, 2016 at ELR 2.30 PM onwards. Guest lecture, Mr. Hudson and Mr. Jayathu participated to the event as resource persons and undergraduates of department of Electrical and Information engineering were the participators of the workshop. Mr. Hudson deliver the lecture on fiber optical technology and submarine optical fiber communication. This workshop was very useful for improve the knowledge of the undergraduate students. Beside he share his experiences and encouraged participators towards self-learning.

School Robotics Seminar

• 24th of June, 2016

EIES of faculty of engineering, University of Ruhuna with the collaboration IET student branch has organized the School Robotics Seminar 2016 in faculty premises. We invited to 8 schools in Galle to this workshop. We started our seminar at IS Seminar room and give them the basic introduction about the seminar and the value and advantages of learning robotics. There we deliver our sessions from very basic level with the aid of practical environment and all the sessions we conducted was interactive sessions and according to syllabus that was guided by the head of the department of electrical and information engineering.
